About Us

At Premium Coffee Cambodia, we celebrate the rich heritage of Cambodian coffee culture. Our beans are carefully grown in the fertile highlands of Mondulkiri and Ratanakiri, nurtured by the country’s unique climate and soil. Every roast reflects the warmth of Cambodian hospitality and the dedication of local farmers who have passed down their craft for generations.
